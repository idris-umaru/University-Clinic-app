Our Mission

The Intelligent Campus Health Management System was developed to simplify and improve access to healthcare services for students and staff within the university campus. It provides users with a modern, smart, and centralized platform to book appointments, chat with an AI health bot, and file complaints with ease.

Key Features

  • Online Appointment Booking
  • AI-Powered Health Assistant
  • Secure Patient-Doctor Communication
  • Firebase Authentication
  • Responsive Dashboard and Role-based Access
  • Complaint Submission and Management
  • Real-time Notifications
  • Data Security and Privacy Compliance
  • Accessible on Multiple Devices
  • Comprehensive User Support
